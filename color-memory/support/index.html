Support

Apple Watch App EN / 日本語

English

Thank you for playing Color Memory. If you have questions, bug reports, or feature requests, please contact us.

How to Play

  • Tap the screen to start.
  • Watch the color sequence.
  • Repeat the sequence by tapping the color pads.
  • After a mistake, your score may appear on the ranking board.

Contact

Please use the support contact/options provided on the App Store listing.

Please include your watchOS version and a short description of the issue when contacting support.

日本語

遊び方

  • 画面をタップして開始します。
  • 表示される色の順番を見ます。
  • 同じ順番で色パッドをタップします。
  • ミスした後、スコアがランキングに表示されます。
